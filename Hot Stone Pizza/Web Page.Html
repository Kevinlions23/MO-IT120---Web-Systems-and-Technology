About:

Welcome to Hot Stone Pizza – where we bring your pizza dreams to life! At Hot Stone Pizza, we believe in the power of choice. That’s why we let you craft the pizza of your dreams with our fully customizable options. Start with our premium, hand-stretched dough, select your favorite sauce, add cheese (or two!), and pile on as many fresh toppings as you like. Every pizza is then carefully baked on a hot stone to achieve a crispy, golden crust with a perfect balance of flavors in every bite.
